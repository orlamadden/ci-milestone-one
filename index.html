About us

Matt’s Café is a family-founded restaurant serving up some of the best Irish cuisine made from locally sourced and sustainable ingredients across the kingdom of Kerry. We are proud to serve humanely raised meat and small farm produce from some of the best producers in the local area. Our restaurant opened its doors in 2011 and has been happily serving the local and wider community since.
